Joylet Support

A gentle daily ritual: draw a little joy, keep a streak, grow your tree, and collect small moments of calm.

Quick tips

  • Draw a joy by shaking your phone or tapping the signing tube.
  • Check in once a day to keep your streak and earn a drop of water for your tree.
  • Water your tree to help it bloom, one flower for every joy you keep.
  • Write your own little joys to add to the tube.
  • Switch between English and 中文 any time with the language button.

Your data

Everything you create stays on your device. Joylet collects nothing. Removing the app deletes all of its data.
