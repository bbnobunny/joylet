Joylet - Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2026

Joylet is built to respect your privacy. It is designed to collect nothing about you.

No data collection

Joylet does not collect, store, or share any personal information. There are no accounts, no sign-up, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party tracking of any kind.

Your content stays on your device

The little joys you draw and keep, your journal notes, your streak, your garden, and your settings are stored only on your device. This information never leaves your device and is never sent to us or to anyone else. If you have device backups enabled (for example iCloud), this data may be included in your own backups, which are controlled entirely by you and your Apple account.

No third parties

Joylet does not include any third-party software development kits that collect data. Nothing you do in the app is transmitted off your device.

Children's privacy

Joylet is suitable for all ages and does not collect data from anyone, including children.

Deleting your data

Removing the app from your device deletes all of its data.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new date above.

Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at bbnobunny@gmail.com.
